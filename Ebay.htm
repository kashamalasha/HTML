how it works

Description: The Servo Eliminator is a small, lightweight solution that eliminates the need for the exhaust servo motor in your Kawasaki (Butterfly Exhaust valve), which is not needed with any aftermarket exhaust system. Typically when you purchase an aftermarket exhaust, the servo motor controlling the restriction valve must remain connected, preventing the FI fault light from illuminating on the dash board. The Servo Eliminator replaces this servo motor with a small, lightweight, and reliable alternative, based on operational amplifier (works as voltage repeater) and some passive electronic components.

Size: The Servo Eliminator frees up valuable room that could be used for power commanders, traction control computers, quick shifters, alarm system or a huge variety of other accessories.

Weight: Half the benefit of that fancy new exhaust system was to save weight, so why not remove that heavy useless motor thatâ€™s spinning around doing nothing under your seat too! The Servo Eliminator saves a lot of weight, in fact it weighs less than an ounce (20grams).

Reliability: The Servo Eliminator electronic are made in the Russia Federation on the finest quality printed circuit boards with the best electrical components. Every unit undergoes extensive functionality and build quality testing before packaging. The factory servo motors are far more likely to fail, and often times do. Many of bikers still have factory exhaust systems, but get tired of replacing the servo motor every year or two due to failures and replace it once and for all with the Servo Eliminator.
